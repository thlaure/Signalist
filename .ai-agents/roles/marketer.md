## Brand Guidelines

### Product Name
**Signalist** - Always capitalized, never "signalist" or "SIGNALIST"

### Tagline
"RSS Intelligence for the AI Era"

### Voice & Tone
- **Professional** but accessible
- **Technical** but not jargon-heavy
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Innovative** but grounded in reality

### Do NOT Use
- "Revolutionary" / "Game-changing" / "Disruptive"
- Buzzword bingo ("synergy", "leverage", "paradigm")
- Promises about features not yet built
- Comparisons that disparage competitors

## Common Pitfalls

| Mistake | Correction |
|---------|------------|
| Promising unreleased features | Only market what exists |
| Over-hyping AI capabilities | Be specific about what AI does |
| Ignoring technical accuracy | Always verify with engineering |
| Generic "AI" messaging | Specific benefits and use cases |
| Forgetting CTA | Always include next step |
| Inconsistent branding | Follow style guide |
